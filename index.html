Avinash's Weather Page

Hello! My name is Avinash Aravind and I study the weather. I'm currently a sophomore majoring in Atmospheric Sciences at Cornell University, and I work on assorted projects related to our atmosphere (and others) when I get a chance. Some of those projects are featured on this site, so feel free to take a look around!

I'm intrigued by pretty much any and all weather phenomena, but some particulars include:

  • Tropical Cyclones
    • TC Formation and environmental conditions
    • Eyewall Replacement Cycles and inner core structure
  • Winter Weather
    • Mesoscale snow bands and frotogenesis events
    • Lake effect snow (proud member of the Finger Lake Effect Snow Fan Club)
  • Modeling
    • High-res modeling outputs of any of the above
    • Probabalistic Modeling of weather events
